🌍 About SustainHub

A capstone project aligned with UN SDG 13 – Climate Action

Selected SDG: Goal 13 – Climate Action

13

Take urgent action to combat climate change and its impacts

SDG 13 calls for urgent action at all levels to minimize the disruptive impacts of climate change. It targets improving resilience and adaptive capacity to climate-related hazards, integrating climate measures into policy, and improving education and awareness about climate change mitigation.

Climate change is one of the most pressing challenges of our time. Rising global temperatures, extreme weather events, melting polar ice, and rising sea levels threaten ecosystems and human communities around the world. India is particularly vulnerable, with heat waves, erratic monsoons, and coastal flooding affecting millions of people.

Problem Statement

What is the problem?

Most individuals are unaware of their daily carbon footprint — the total greenhouse gases emitted through their everyday habits like electricity usage, transportation, and food choices.

Where does this problem exist?

This problem is global, but particularly significant in fast-growing economies like India where urbanization, rising incomes, and energy demand are rapidly increasing per-capita emissions.

Who is affected?

Everyone — individuals, families, and communities — who contribute to emissions without realizing it. Future generations bear the heaviest burden of unaddressed climate change today.

Why is it serious?

The IPCC warns we must cut global emissions by 45% by 2030 to limit warming to 1.5°C. Without individual action at scale, this target becomes impossible to achieve through policy alone.

What if it is not solved?

Continued high emissions will lead to more frequent extreme weather, food insecurity, water scarcity, displacement of coastal populations, and irreversible ecological damage.

Proposed Solution – SustainHub

SustainHub is a web application that empowers individuals to understand, track, and reduce their personal carbon footprint. It bridges the gap between abstract climate data and concrete personal action.

🧮

Carbon Calculator

Users answer simple questions about their energy, transport, food, and waste habits. The system estimates their annual CO₂ emissions in tons.

📊

Progress Tracker

Users log their footprint over time and visualize trends through charts. Achievements reward consistent tracking and improvement.

🏆

Eco Challenges

Gamified weekly challenges like "No-Car Week" and "Meatless Monday" encourage behavioral change in a fun, engaging way.

💡

Sustainability Tips

Categorized actionable tips on energy, transport, food, and waste help users take targeted action in the areas where they emit the most.
